8. Risk Value Determination

Internal Rule: Risk level is derived from maximum impact and attack feasibility. The score is calculated as Impact(0-4) + Feasibility(0-4), then mapped to Low, Medium, High, or Critical.

  • 0-2: Low
  • 3-4: Medium
  • 5-6: High
  • 7-8: Critical

9. Risk Treatment Decision

For each identified threat scenario, the recommended treatment is shown in the table above. High or Critical risks should be reduced and traced to cybersecurity goals. Medium risks may be reduced or retained with an explicit rationale. Low risks may be retained with documented assumptions and monitoring.
